Shakshuka

A tasty dish made with eggs, tomatoes, garlic and onions

Ingridietns

  • eggs
  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • garlic
  • spices to your liking

Steps

  1. Add onions and garlic to pan, saute until golden and fragrant
  2. once golden, add tomatoes in all its forms. add spices as well
  3. add eggs
  4. cover lid, wait 5m
  5. shakshuka ready
